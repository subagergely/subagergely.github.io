About me

I’m Gergely Suba from Budapest, Hungary. I finished my BSc, MSc and PhD studies in Computer Engineering major at BUTE.

Fields of interest:

  • model-based software development
  • domain-specific languages (DSL)
  • code generation
  • high-level sythesis (HLS)
  • Eclipse based tool development

I currently work at Prolan Zrt. as head of tool development department. Since 2017 the company built a hybrid-electronic railway interlocking system (PRORIS) with 60 colleagues working on it. Our team implemented a DSL for railway interlocking algorithms, and another one to describe the static and dynamic (state machine) aspects of network protocols. We’ve also been developing a CAD software to design the interlocking aspects of railway stations.
